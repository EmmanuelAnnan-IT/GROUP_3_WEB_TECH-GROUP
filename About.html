Mr. Francis Xavier Kofi Akotoye

Profile

Mr. Francis Xavier Kofi Akotoye is a Lecturer at the University of Cape Coast, Ghana, under Computer Science Information Technology. He obtained his MEng. degree in Computer Science & Technology from Hunan University, Changsha, China, from Sept. 2004 to 2007, and a B.A. degree in Secretaryship from the University of Cape Coast, Ghana, from Aug. 1997 to June 2000.

Mr. Akotoye has been at the University of Cape Coast since 2000. He is a former Patron for the Computer Science and Information Technology Students Association (CITSA).
He has an interest in Computer Security, Cloud Computing, Mobile Computing, and the Internet of Things (IoT).

Mr. Kofi Akotoye has published numerous papers in international journals and conferences.
He is also an active member of the following affiliations:
◽ Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE)
◽ Information Technology Association of Ghana (ITAG)
◽ Internet Society (ISOC)
