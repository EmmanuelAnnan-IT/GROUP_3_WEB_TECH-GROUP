Mr. Francis Xavier Kofi Akotoye

Profile

Mr. Francis Xavier Kofi Akotoye is a Lecturer of the University of Cape Coast, Ghana, under Computer Science Information Technology. He obtained his MEng. degree in Computer Science & Technology from the Hunan University, Changsha, Peoples' Republic of China from Sept.,2004 to 2007 and B.A. degree in Secretaryship from the University of Cape Coast, Ghana, from Aug., 1997 to June, 2000.

Mr. Akotoye has been at the University of Cape Coast since 2000. He's a former Patron for the the Computer Science and Infomation Technology Students Association(CITSA).
He has interest in Computer Security, Cloud Computing, Mobile Computing, and Internet of Things(IoT)

Mr. Kofi Akotoye has published numerous papers in international journals and conferences.
He is also an active member of the following Affilliations;
◽Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineer (IEEE)
◽ Information Technology Association of Ghana (ITAG)
◽Internet Society (ISOC)
